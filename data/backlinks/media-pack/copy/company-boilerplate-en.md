# Voyager Balloons EU

Voyager Balloons EU operates sunrise hot air balloon flights over Segovia, with views of the Alcazar, the Cathedral, the aqueduct, the historic city and the surrounding mountains. The experience is designed for travellers looking for a memorable activity near Madrid, couples, families, groups, companies and experience gifts.

The team has more than 25 years of hot air balloon flying experience. Safety, weather assessment and passenger care are central to the experience. After landing, passengers enjoy a cava toast.
