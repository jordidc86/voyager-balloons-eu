Why international travellers choose Voyager Balloons

Ballooning is weather-dependent, so clarity matters. We confirm operational details according to forecast, wind and safety conditions, and if the pilot cancels for safety or weather, we arrange a new date at no extra cost.

The experience takes place at sunrise and includes the balloon flight, a cava toast after landing and a flight diploma. No physical office visit is needed: bookings are handled online and by phone or WhatsApp.
