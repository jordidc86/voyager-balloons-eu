Quick answer

Can you fly in a hot air balloon in Segovia?

Yes. Voyager Balloons operates sunrise hot air balloon flights in Segovia, one of the most scenic ballooning areas near Madrid. The experience starts from €120 per passenger and is confirmed according to weather and wind conditions.
