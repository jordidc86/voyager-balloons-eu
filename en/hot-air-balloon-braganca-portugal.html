Why Braganca is a special ballooning destination

Braganca combines open horizons, natural landscapes and a strong Portuguese identity. A sunrise flight gives visitors a calm, panoramic view of the region, always shaped by wind and weather.

A hot air balloon does not follow a fixed route. The pilot uses wind layers and chooses the safest option for the conditions of the day.
